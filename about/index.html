Our History

We, a group of Engineers, experience in Industrial Automation field to future Embedded Technology (IOT & Robotics) for over a decade, stepped into building technologies. Our vision is predominantly focused into bridging the GAP between Industrial Automation and IOT. We understand both. We are into making of both ready and customize; stand-alone embedded system to smart IOT modules. We are into helping people and industries for smart control, monitoring, data logging and analyzing any system.
